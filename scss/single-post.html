A Closer Look At Our Grasshopper Pie

by our chef Raihan 216 176

Fried Grasshopper is a traditional food made from grasshoppers. This food does look strange because the basic ingredient itself is Grasshopper. But apparently this Grasshopper has a delicious taste and also nutritious. This Fried Grasshopper is one of the very famous traditional foods from Gunung Kidul, Yogyakarta.

because of that we want to adapt local food, so young people can now taste it, by making pies made from anti-mainstream. take a long time to do research on the taste of our new pie can be accepted by the general public we went to Gunung Kidul, Yogyakarta to ask for recipes and properties contained in grasshoppers that we asked local residents.

That’s not to say you’ll have the exact same thing if you stop by: the restaurant’s menus change constantly, based on seasonal ingredients.
